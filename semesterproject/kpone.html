KPONE ON SEA

There is no better way to refer to Kpone, than the name, "Kpone on Sea." This is the nickname the indigens and residents have given to their beloved home, which is a perfect description of the location of the town. Kpone is found in the coastal region of Ghana called Greater Accra, which is occupied predominantly by the ethnic group known as the "Ga". The traditional profession of the Kpone is fishing, which is typical of the Ga tribe who settled in the coastal areas of the country.

Source of Ghana's Revenue

Many argue, with good reason, that Kpone is the nation's highest revenue generator. This is owing to the fact that many large companies, ranging from food processing companies like Nestle, to Car companies such as Toyota, have situated their factories in the Kpone district. These are but a few of the many that are in the area, and many more are being built. It is apparent that Kpone is highly attractive to investors from all over because of the abundance of land and other natural resources it is blessed with.
