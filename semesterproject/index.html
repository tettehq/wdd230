WELCOME

The Kpone Chamber of Commerce is an association of businesses, firms and industries in the Kpone Katamanso district with the aim of protecting and promoting these commercial organizations and ultimately, accelerating the economic and social development of the district.

The chamber represents the collective voice of member businesses who are commited to playing leading roles in nurturing the struggling but potentious economy of the Kpone Katamanso municipality. As a support organization, we provide member businesses with resources and facilities to help them expand and contribute something meaningful to the economy. Additionally, we serve as a link between these businesses and the local government.

Our vision is to foster an unbreakable bond of cooperation and unity among the various commercial institutions in the district, and to harness the power of this bond to drive the economy.
